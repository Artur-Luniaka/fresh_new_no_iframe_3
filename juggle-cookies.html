Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit our game website. They help us provide you with a better gaming experience by remembering your preferences and game progress.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Game progress and save states
  • High scores and achievements
  • User preferences and settings
  • Performance monitoring and analytics
  • Authentication and security

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for basic game functionality and cannot be disabled. These cookies ensure proper game saving and loading.

Performance Cookies

Help us understand how players interact with our game to improve gameplay experience.

Functionality Cookies

Remember your preferences and customizations for enhanced gameplay.

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your device and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. However, if you do this, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit our game website, and some features may not work.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
